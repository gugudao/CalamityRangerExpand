Calamity Ranger Expansion is a mod that provides several extra ranger ammunitions and accessories, in order to provides more options for rangers and play for fun, instead of doing strict researches about "Who is the strongest equipment set now" or "How overpowered this thing is" all day long;

About the recipes: 
Please use the Recipe Browser mod.

[h1]About ammunitions effects:[/h1] 
In the game there are simple text descriptions. As for their specific functions, I hope you guys can explore and discover them by yourself
